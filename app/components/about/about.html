About Did or Wish


I use this app to track things that I did instead of things to do. Because when I miss things in my to do list I feel like a failure. However, with this idea of logging things I did, things I wish I would have done, and things that I shouldn't do but did, I will be able to have a better reflection on my productivity and planning. I came across this idea in The Power of No by James Altucher, and I just thought that it would be cool to build an app for that.
